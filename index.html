About

Hey,my name's DJ Lott.I'm a front end developer based out of Tampa,Florida.As you may have seen above,I'm also a multi-instrumentalist and photographer.However,we'll get to that in a moment.I would attribute my foray into front end development to a penchant for brilliant design.

Indeed,the staggering realm of possibilities within the ecosystem of development was enough to pique my most sincere interest.I began by joining freeCodeCamp and utilizing Codecademy(which I still use).I'd be remiss not to mention Google,which has perhaps been the most indispensible tool of all the ones I've mentioned.I'm currently getting my hands dirty with well known technologies such as jQuery,React,and Angular.

As far as music is concerned,I'm a drummer,trombonist,and vocalist.I've recently taken up acoustic and electric guitar as well.Drums are my main instrument with eight years behind the kit.I've done a few electronic projects and plan to publish many more in the future.
